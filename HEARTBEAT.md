# HEARTBEAT.md

## Purpose
Periodic maintenance, refinement, and quality control for the prose-research workspace.

## Execution Cadence
- Heartbeat tasks are intended to run once daily.
- Perform research memory consolidation and refinement at a low frequency.
- Avoid repeated or redundant maintenance within the same day.
- Check `README.md` for changes at each heartbeat; if changed, re-read and append a short summary to `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.

## Research Memory Maintenance
- Review recent files in `memory/`
- Identify repeated patterns or durable research insights
- Promote high-value learnings into `MEMORY.md`
- Remove outdated or redundant entries from `MEMORY.md`
- Keep `MEMORY.md` concise and high-signal

## Output Quality Control
- Check that outputs follow the standard research template
- Ensure inclusion of:
  - structured sections
  - methods
  - results
  - limitations
  - PubMed / PMC links when available
- Detect verbosity drift or loss of structure

## Search Optimization
- Identify which query patterns consistently yield strong results
- Flag low-yield queries for de-prioritization
- Update preferred search patterns in `MEMORY.md` when they prove durable

## System Discipline
- Favor deterministic inspection over guesswork
- Do not rewrite core operating files unless a durable improvement is identified
- Promote stable lessons into `MEMORY.md` only when they are reusable
